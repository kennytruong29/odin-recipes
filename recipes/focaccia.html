Focaccia

If you are intimidated by bread baking, this is the recipe I suggest making first, both for its simplicity and flavor.


Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Yeast
  • Salt
  • Water
  • Olive Oil
  • Rosemary or other seasonings

Step-By-Step Instructions

  1. Whisk flour, salt and yeast in a bowl. Then add water
  2. Stir the mixture
  3. Slick the dough with olive oil
  4. Cover the bowl and stick it in the fridge immediately
  5. Leave it in fridge to rise for 12 to 18 hours
  6. Deflate dough and transfer to a non-stick pan
  7. Leave dough for 2 to 4 hours
  8. Dimple the dough by using olive oil and sea salt. Flaky sea salt recommended
  9. Sprinkle rosemary over dough
  10. Bake at 425 degrees fahrenheit for 25 minutes
